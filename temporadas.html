THE SEASONS

SEASON 1

When calm comes to an end

The first season begins with the mysterious disappearance of Will Byers in the small town of Hawkins. While his friends begin a desperate search, a girl with supernatural abilities known as Eleven appears and joins them, revealing clues about what really happened. At the same time, Will’s mother, Joyce, is convinced that her son is still alive and tries to communicate with him through strange phenomena.

As the investigation moves forward, the existence of a secret laboratory is uncovered, one that has opened a portal to a parallel dimension known as the Upside Down, from which a terrifying creature emerges: the Demogorgon. The season blends mystery, tension and emotion, culminating in a struggle to rescue Will and close the breach between both worlds, leaving consequences that will shape the future of Hawkins and its inhabitants.

SEASON 2

Monsters that live within

One year after the initial events, Will Byers struggles to return to normal life after coming back from the Upside Down, although he continues to suffer from disturbing visions and episodes. These signs reveal that the parallel dimension is still connected to Hawkins and that a far more powerful entity, known as the Mind Flayer, is threatening to invade the real world.

While the laboratory attempts to contain the situation, the group of friends faces new creatures called Demodogs, which spread rapidly throughout the town. Meanwhile, Eleven begins a personal journey to discover her origins and the true extent of her powers. Through a collective effort to close the rifts between both worlds and free Will from the Mind Flayer’s control, the season makes it clear that the danger of the Upside Down is far from over.

SEASON 3

Allies in the shadows

During the summer in Hawkins, the opening of a new shopping mall seems to bring normality and entertainment, but it soon becomes the stage for a new threat. Secret experiments reactivate the connection to the Upside Down, allowing the Mind Flayer to manifest physically in the real world. At the same time, a hidden Russian base beneath the mall attempts to open a portal to the parallel dimension.

As the group splits up to investigate different events, several townspeople are taken over by the Mind Flayer, forming an increasingly powerful and dangerous creature. The season reaches its climax in a decisive battle to close the portal and stop the invasion, with deep emotional consequences for the characters and changes that will shape the future of Hawkins.

SEASON 4

Fear has a name

The fourth season introduces an even darker and more personal threat to Hawkins. A series of mysterious deaths reveals the presence of Vecna, a powerful entity from the Upside Down that attacks its victims psychologically, feeding on their deepest fears and traumas. His crimes spread panic throughout the town and force the group to face a completely different kind of terror.

The final confrontation exposes the connection between Vecna, the Upside Down and the events that have unfolded since the first season, revealing that everything has been linked from the very beginning. Hawkins is left deeply scarred, both physically and emotionally, with wounds that are difficult to heal and significant losses. These events set the stage for a final conflict in which the balance between both worlds is more threatened than ever.

SEASON 5

The circle closes

The fifth and final season of Stranger Things takes place after the devastating events that have left Hawkins fractured by the rifts opening into the Upside Down, making it impossible to ignore the threat that has always lurked beneath the surface. As the town lives in fear and uncertainty, the group of friends reunites once again with a single goal: to stop Vecna for good, whose influence continues to spread and connect both worlds.

Eleven must face her most powerful enemy yet, as well as the consequences of her own decisions, while Will Byers takes on a central role due to his deep and persistent connection to the Upside Down. Throughout the season, the origins of this dimension and the bonds between the characters are explored, leading to a final confrontation that will decide the fate of Hawkins and close the cycle that began with Will’s disappearance on November 6, 1983.
